# Grand Horizon Hotel — Facilities & Services

## Swimming Pool
- Outdoor heated pool, open year-round
- Hours: 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Towels provided poolside
- Children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult
- Poolside bar open 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM

## Fitness Center
- 24/7 access with room key
- Equipment: treadmills, ellipticals, stationary bikes, free weights, cable machines
- Yoga mats and resistance bands available
- Personal trainer available by appointment ($80/session)

## Spa & Wellness
- Hours: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Services: Swedish massage, deep tissue, hot stone, facials, body wraps
- Pricing: massages from $120/60min, facials from $95
- Sauna and steam room included with any spa treatment
- Booking recommended 24 hours in advance

## Restaurant — The Horizon Grill
- Breakfast: 6:30 AM - 10:30 AM (buffet $28, a la carte available)
- Lunch: 12:00 PM - 2:30 PM
- Dinner: 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM
- Cuisine: International, with vegetarian/vegan/gluten-free options
- Smart casual dress code for dinner
- Reservations recommended for dinner

## Bar — Skyline Lounge
- Hours: 4:00 PM - 12:00 AM (Friday-Saturday until 1:00 AM)
- Craft cocktails, wine, beer, spirits
- Happy hour: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM (20% off drinks)
- Live music: Friday and Saturday evenings 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM

## Room Service
- Available 24/7
- Full dinner menu 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM
- Late-night menu 10:00 PM - 6:00 AM
- Delivery fee: $5 per order
- Average delivery time: 30-45 minutes

## Business Center
- Hours: 24/7 with room key
- Printing, scanning, faxing available
- 2 private meeting rooms (capacity: 8 each, $100/hour)
- High-speed Wi-Fi throughout hotel (complimentary)

## Wi-Fi
- Complimentary standard Wi-Fi for all guests (50 Mbps)
- Premium Wi-Fi upgrade: $10/day (200 Mbps)

## Concierge Services
- Available: 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM at front desk
- Airport shuttle: $35 one-way (reserve 24 hours ahead)
- Car rental arrangements
- Restaurant reservations and activity bookings
- Local attraction recommendations and tickets

## Laundry & Dry Cleaning
- Same-day service if submitted before 9:00 AM
- Express service (4-hour): 50% surcharge
- Self-service laundry room on Level 1 ($3 wash, $3 dry)
- Ironing board and iron in every room
