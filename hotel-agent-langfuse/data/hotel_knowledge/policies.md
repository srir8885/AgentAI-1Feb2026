## Check-in / Check-out
- Check-in time: 3:00 PM
- Check-out time: 11:00 AM
- Early check-in available from 12:00 PM (subject to availability, $50 fee)
- Late check-out until 2:00 PM ($50 fee), until 6:00 PM ($100 fee)
- Express check-in available via the Grand Horizon mobile app

## Cancellation Policy
- Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in
- Cancellations within 48 hours: one night's charge applies
- No-shows: full stay amount charged
- Group bookings (5+ rooms): 7-day cancellation window

## Payment
- Accepted: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- A hold of $100/night is placed on the card at check-in for incidentals
- Final bill settled at check-out; receipt emailed automatically
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

## Pet Policy
- Dogs and cats welcome (max 2 pets per room)
- Pet fee: $75 per stay (non-refundable)
- Pets must be leashed in common areas
- Pet-friendly rooms available on floors 1-3 only
- Service animals: no fee, all rooms

## Smoking
- The hotel is 100% smoke-free indoors
- Designated smoking areas on the ground floor patio
- $250 cleaning fee for smoking in non-designated areas

## Parking
- Self-parking: $25/night
- Valet parking: $45/night
- Electric vehicle charging stations available (4 spots, complimentary)
- Oversized vehicles: $40/night self-parking

## Children
- Children under 12 stay free when sharing parents' room
- Cribs and rollaway beds available on request (complimentary)
- Kids' club operates daily 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM (ages 4-12)
