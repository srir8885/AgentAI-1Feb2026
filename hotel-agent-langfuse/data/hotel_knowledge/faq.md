# Grand Horizon Hotel — Frequently Asked Questions

## Q: Is breakfast included?
A: Breakfast is not included in the standard room rate. Buffet breakfast is available at The Horizon Grill for $28 per person. Some promotional rates and suite bookings include complimentary breakfast — check your booking confirmation for details.

## Q: What is the Wi-Fi password?
A: Connect to "GrandHorizon-Guest" network. You'll be prompted to enter your last name and room number. No additional password needed.

## Q: Do you offer airport transportation?
A: Yes, we offer airport shuttle service for $35 one-way. Please reserve through the concierge at least 24 hours in advance. Ride-share pickup is available at the hotel's main entrance.

## Q: Is there a loyalty program?
A: Yes! Grand Horizon Rewards members earn 10 points per $1 spent. Benefits include room upgrades, late check-out, and exclusive rates. Sign up free at the front desk or on our website.

## Q: Can I store luggage before check-in or after check-out?
A: Absolutely. Our bell desk offers complimentary luggage storage for guests on their check-in/check-out day. Extended storage is available at $10/bag/day.

## Q: What is the minimum age to check in?
A: Guests must be at least 21 years old to check in. A valid government-issued photo ID is required.

## Q: Do you have adjoining rooms?
A: Yes, adjoining and connecting rooms are available. Please request when booking to ensure availability. We cannot guarantee adjoining rooms for walk-in or last-minute reservations.

## Q: Is the hotel wheelchair accessible?
A: Yes, the hotel is fully ADA compliant. We offer accessible rooms, ramps, elevators, and accessible pool entry. Please let us know your needs when booking.

## Q: What is your lost and found policy?
A: Found items are held for 90 days. Contact the front desk with a description of the lost item. Shipping of found items costs $15-$30 depending on size.

## Q: Can I host an event at the hotel?
A: Yes! We have event spaces for 10-250 guests. Our events team can help with weddings, conferences, and private dining. Contact events@grandhorizon.com for details and availability.
